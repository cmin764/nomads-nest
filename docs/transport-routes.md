# Transport Routes: Ayia Napa

Reference doc for all bus and transport routes relevant to Nomad's Nest guests. Sources: guest WhatsApp conversations (Mar 2026), official InterCity schedule PDFs v13.6 (effective 13 June 2026, provided by owner), osea.com.cy live route pages (checked Jun 2026), kapnosairportshuttle.com route/fare pages (checked Jun 2026), Pame route exports (Apr 2026), Google Maps / Moovit / CyprusByBus (Apr 2026).

Prices and timetables change seasonally. Always link guests to official sources rather than quoting exact departure times.

## From the Airports

### Larnaca Airport (LCA)

**Option 1: Kapnos Airport Shuttle (most convenient)**
- Direct Larnaca Airport to Ayia Napa. No transfers.
- Price: EUR 12 one way (adult), EUR 5 (child). Confirmed from kapnosairportshuttle.com/routes fare table, Jun 2026.
- Drop-off points: Ayia Napa Square (Arch. Makariou III 19), Nissi Avenue 80 (in front of Sunwing Sandy Bay), Pernera Avenue (Windmills area), Protaras Avenue (McDonald's).
- Book online at kapnosairportshuttle.com. Booking closes 60 min before departure.
- Free cancellation up to 24h before departure.
- Note: Seats sell out in peak season. Book in advance.

**Option 2: Pame Bus 425 + InterCity (cheapest)**
- Step 1: **Pame Bus 425** from Larnaca Airport to Larnaca Central Station. Runs roughly every 20-30 min from early morning to late evening. About 25 min. Fare: approx. EUR 1.50-2.
  - Board at the airport bus stop (ground floor arrivals exit).
  - Get off at Larnaca Central Station (Google Maps: https://maps.app.goo.gl/J3nJyZ4K11eQdTCL7).
  - Does not run overnight. For early morning or late night flights, use Kapnos or taxi.
- Step 2: Walk ~3 min to the **Nicolaides Building on Archiep. Makariou Ave.** (current InterCity stop; Finikoudes is temporarily under renovation). Take **InterCity Bus Line 43** to Ayia Napa Monastery. EUR 5, ~55-65 min.
  - Line 43 is the main Larnaca--Ayia Napa--Paralimni InterCity service. Arrives near Nisi Ave. (Ayia Napa).
  - Runs on an irregular schedule, roughly 06:00-21:30 (17 departures/day on weekdays). See live timetable: intercity-buses.com/en/routes/larnaca-ayia-napa-paralimni-paralimni-ayia-napa-larnaca/
- Total: approx. EUR 6-7. Total time: ~1.5-2 hrs depending on wait.

**Warning: Taxi / Bolt**
- Larnaca Airport to Ayia Napa typically costs EUR 70-90. Can be higher at night or during peak season.

### Paphos Airport (PFO)

**Option 1: Kapnos Airport Shuttle**
- Kapnos covers PFO via a multiride connection: EUR 30 total one way to Ayia Napa (confirmed Jun 2026). Check kapnosairportshuttle.com for current prices and for direct PFO--Ayia Napa availability.

**Option 2: InterCity Bus from Paphos (very inflexible)**
- One bus per day departing Paphos Karavella at 16:00, arriving Ayia Napa Monastery (via Larnaca): EUR 10. Only viable if you land early enough in the afternoon. Always check the current schedule at intercity-buses.com.

**Warning: Taxi / Bolt**
- Paphos Airport to Ayia Napa: EUR 120-150+.

## InterCity Buses (intercity-buses.com)

Operator: InterCity Buses (OSEL). Freephone: 8000 77 89 (08:00-21:00).
Cash only to driver for one-way. Return/weekly/monthly via Motion Card.
Prices: confirmed from official fare table, updated Apr 2025.

### Line 43 / Larnaca -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni
- The main InterCity service between Larnaca and the Famagusta district. Fully official line listed in Moovit, CyprusByBus, and third-party planners.
- **Larnaca departure stop:** Nicolaides Building on Archiep. Makariou Ave. (current stop; Finikoudes is temporarily under renovation). ~3 min walk from [Larnaca Central Station](https://maps.app.goo.gl/J3nJyZ4K11eQdTCL7). Historical Finikoudes stop: [map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/B94PCqvWHtefwnd9A)
- **Ayia Napa arrival:** Nisi Ave. - Katalymmata area, then continues to Ayia Napa Monastery and Paralimni.
- **Reverse departure:** Nisi Ave. - Vatheias Gonias area.
- **Price:** EUR 5 one-way, EUR 9 return (paper Motion card), EUR 40 weekly / EUR 90 monthly / EUR 900 yearly (plastic Motion card only). Confirmed against the official InterCity schedule PDF (v13.6, effective 13 June 2026).
- **Duration:** approx. 1hr 15min end-to-end (Larnaca to Paralimni). Ayia Napa Monastery is reached sooner, roughly 55-65 min from Larnaca.
- **Frequency:** irregular, not a clean interval. Larnaca to Ayia Napa & Paralimni: weekdays 05:55-21:30 (17 departures/day), weekends 06:00-21:30. Gaps range from 30 to about 95 min depending on time of day. Always check the live timetable rather than relying on any static list: intercity-buses.com/en/routes/larnaca-ayia-napa-paralimni-paralimni-ayia-napa-larnaca/
- **Key stops:** Larnaca Port, Jumbo (Ammochostou), New Famagusta station (Super Action), **Ayia Napa Monastery** (closest to apartment, ~10 min walk), Nissi Beach, Makronissos, Water World Waterpark.

### Nicosia -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni
- **Price:** EUR 6 one-way, EUR 11 return, EUR 55 weekly.
- **Duration:** approx. 1.5-2 hrs.
- **Nicosia departure:** Solomos Square.
- **Frequency:** roughly every 1-2 hrs, first bus around 07:15, last around 21:15. Check live timetable: intercity-buses.com/en/routes/nicosia-ayia-napa-paralimni-ayia-napa-paralimni-nicosia/
- **Key stops:** Nicosia, New Famagusta station, **Ayia Napa Monastery**, Nissi Beach, Water World Waterpark.

### Paphos -- Larnaca -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni
- **Price:** EUR 10 one-way, EUR 19 return (paper Motion card), EUR 100 weekly / EUR 210 monthly / EUR 2100 yearly (plastic Motion card only). Confirmed against the official InterCity schedule PDF (v13.6, effective 13 June 2026).
- **Duration:** approx. 3 hrs for the full Paphos-Paralimni route (not 2 hrs as previously noted here).
- **One bus per day**, both directions:
  - Paphos to Larnaca/Ayia Napa/Paralimni: departs New Terminal Pervola-Karavella at 16:00. Also passes Protaras.
  - Paralimni/Ayia Napa to Larnaca/Paphos: departs Paralimni at 07:30, stops at Nikolaidis Building in Larnaca around 09:00.
- Only viable for guests landing at Paphos early enough in the afternoon (16:00 departure). Check current schedule: intercity-buses.com/en/routes/paralimni-ayia-napa-larnaca-paphos-paphos-larnaca-ayia-napa-paralimni/
- Stops same as Larnaca route on the Larnaca-Ayia Napa leg.

## Pame Bus 425 (Larnaca Airport ↔ City)

Operator: Pame / Cyprus Public Transport (Larnaca urban service).
Route: Dekeleia -- beach hotels -- Larnaca Station -- Finikoudes -- Makenzy -- Airport (and reverse).

- Runs roughly every 20-30 min during the day, in both directions.
- First/last bus: early morning to late evening (approx. 06:05 - 22:55). Does not run overnight.
- Airport → Larnaca Central Station: ~24 min. Then walk ~3 min to the Nicolaides Building on Archiep. Makariou Ave. for InterCity pickup (Finikoudes is temporarily under renovation).
- For early morning or very late night flights, do not rely on 425. Use Kapnos or taxi.
- Live timetable: publictransport.com.cy (agency: Pame, route 425) or Moovit.

## OSEA Local/Regional Buses (osea.com.cy)

Operator: OSEA (Famagusta district). Freephone: 8000 5200.
Timetable PDFs at osea.com.cy/en/bus-routes/ (updated seasonally).

### Ayia Napa area circles (local beach hopping)
- **201 (West):** Central Bus Station (opposite Pierre Anne Hotel) -- Ayia Napa Square -- Nissi Avenue -- Musan Museum -- Vathia Gonia Beach -- Nissi Beach -- Landa Beach -- Makronissos Beach -- Ayia Napa Marina -- Waterpark -- back. Loop route. Confirmed against osea.com.cy, Jun 2026.
- **202 (East):** Central Bus Station (opposite Pierre Anne Hotel) -- Faros Hotel/Casino -- Ayia Napa Square -- Tefkrou Anthia St. -- M. Kasialou St. -- Kryou Nerou -- Ammos tou Kampouri Beach -- Love Bridge -- Melissi Hotel -- Thalassa Museum -- back. Loop route. Note: the live osea.com.cy page returned a truncated stop list on last check (Jun 2026); Love Bridge and Thalassa Museum carried over from an earlier full read of the route, worth re-confirming next update.

### Ayia Napa to Protaras / Paralimni (coastal)
- **101:** Ayia Napa Waterpark -- Cape Greco -- Konnos -- Protaras -- Pernera -- Paralimni.
- **102:** Reverse of 101.
- **501/502:** Deryneia -- Agia Thekla Beach -- Ayia Napa Marina -- Waterpark -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni (both directions).
- **706:** Xylotympou -- Dasaki -- Avgorou -- Frenaros -- Deryneia -- Paralimni -- Protaras -- Konnos. Inland villages route, does not pass through Ayia Napa town.

### Ayia Napa to Larnaca (regional, two-way)
- **711:** Paralimni -- Kapparis -- Protaras -- Konnos -- Ayia Napa Marina -- coast -- Xylofagou -- Ormideia -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way route. Also serves Municipal Library of Larnaka stop (direction: toward Larnaca). PDF: https://osea.com.cy/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/711.pdf
- **712:** Paralimni -- inland villages -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way.
- **714:** Paralimni -- Liopetri -- Dhekelia -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way.

**Note:** 711/712/714 terminate at Metropolis Mall, not at Finikoudes or the airport. For airport transfers, use InterCity Line 43 + Pame 425.

## Smart Cards

Cyprus-wide "Motion" smart card system covers OSEA, Pame, and InterCity. Non-Personalised
Plastic Smart Card: EUR 5, issued at local points of sale, rechargeable, e-wallet holds up to
EUR 50. One free transfer to a different route within 60 minutes of first validation. Cash is
still the only option for one-way tickets bought directly from the driver. Details:
publictransport.com.cy/cms/page/travel-cards
