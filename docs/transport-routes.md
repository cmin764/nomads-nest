# Transport Routes: Ayia Napa

Reference doc for all bus and transport routes relevant to Nomad's Nest guests. Sources: guest WhatsApp conversations (Mar 2026), intercity-buses.com schedules (prices updated Apr 2025), osea.com.cy timetables (Apr-May 2026), kapnosairportshuttle.com, Pame route exports (Apr 2026), Google Maps (Apr 2026).

---

## From the Airports

### Larnaca Airport (LCA)

**Option 1: Kapnos Airport Shuttle (most convenient)**
- Direct Larnaca Airport to Ayia Napa. No transfers.
- Price: EUR 12 (LCA → Ayia Napa).
- Drop-off points: Ayia Napa Square (Arch. Makariou III 19), Nissi Avenue 80 (in front of Sunwing Sandy Bay), Pernera Avenue (Windmills area), Protaras Avenue (McDonald's).
- Book online at kapnosairportshuttle.com. Booking closes 60 min before departure.
- Free cancellation up to 24h before departure.
- Note: Seats sell out in peak season. Book in advance.

**Option 2: Pame Bus 425 + InterCity (cheapest)**
- Step 1: **Pame Bus 425** from Larnaca Airport to Larnaca city. Every 30 min, from 06:30 to midnight. ~25 min to Finikoudes / Arch. Makariou stop. Fare: ~EUR 1.50-2.
  - Board at the airport bus stop (ground floor arrivals exit).
  - Get off at "Grigori Afxentiou Ave. - Foinikoudes 2" or "Archiep. Makariou C´ Ave. - Filiou Tsigaridi 1".
  - Reference stop (Google Maps): https://maps.app.goo.gl/B94PCqvWHtefwnd9A
- Step 2: **InterCity Bus** (line 43 or regular Larnaca--Ayia Napa service) from Arch. Makariou / Filiou Tsigaridi to Ayia Napa. EUR 5, ~52-55 min.
  - Line 43 is a newer InterCity service appearing in Google Maps (not yet in official InterCity timetables as of Apr 2026). Departs from Arch. Makariou C´ Ave. - Filiou Tsigaridi. Arrives at Nisi Ave. (Ayia Napa).
  - Regular InterCity Larnaca--Ayia Napa service also stops here. Roughly hourly, 05:55-21:30.
- Total: ~EUR 6-7. Total time: ~1.5-2 hrs depending on wait.

**Warning: Taxi / Bolt**
- Larnaca Airport to Ayia Napa costs approximately EUR 80-100. Not recommended unless splitting with a group.

**Note on Bus 425 going TO the airport:**
Bus 425 FROM Larnaca Station toward the airport runs only 9 trips (06:05, 06:35, 07:05, 07:35, 08:35, 15:20, 15:55, 16:25, 16:50). Very limited. For departure day, Kapnos or taxi is more reliable.

### Paphos Airport (PFO)

**Option 1: Kapnos Airport Shuttle**
- Kapnos covers PFO, but the leg PFO → LCA costs EUR 18. Then LCA → Ayia Napa = EUR 12. Total: EUR 30.
- Alternatively, book a direct PFO → Ayia Napa route if available on kapnosairportshuttle.com.

**Option 2: InterCity Bus (cheapest but very inflexible)**
- Daily direct Paphos Karavella → Paralimni (via Larnaca, stops at Ayia Napa Monastery): EUR 10. Departs Paphos at 07:30. One departure per day. Only practical if arriving very early.

**Warning: Taxi / Bolt**
- Paphos Airport to Ayia Napa is significantly more than from Larnaca. Expect EUR 120-150+.

## InterCity Buses (intercity-buses.com)

Operator: InterCity Buses (OSEL). Freephone: 8000 77 89 (08:00-21:00).
Cash only to driver for one-way. Return/weekly/monthly via Motion Card.

### Line 43 (Larnaca -- Ayia Napa, newer route)
- Appears in Google Maps as of Apr 2026 under "INTERCITY". Not yet in official intercity-buses.com timetables.
- Larnaca departure stop: Arch. Makariou C´ Ave. - Filiou Tsigaridi (same as regular InterCity stop, Stop ID 21417).
- Ayia Napa arrival: Nisi Ave. - Katalymmata area.
- Duration: ~52 min (9 stops), ~55 min reverse (8 stops).
- Reverse departure: Nisi Ave. - Vatheias Gonias (Stop ID 40112).
- Price: EUR 5.

### Larnaca -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni (regular service)
- **Price:** EUR 5 one-way, EUR 9 return, EUR 40 weekly.
- **Duration:** ~75 min.
- **Larnaca departure:** Nicolaides Building (Arch. Makariou / Filiou Tsigaridi). Also: "Bus Stop Finikoudes CPT Larnaka / Intercity Bustop" (https://maps.app.goo.gl/B94PCqvWHtefwnd9A).
- **Weekday departures:** 05:55, 07:30, 09:30, 10:00, 11:00, 12:30, 13:30, 14:30, 15:00, 15:45, 17:15, 18:00, 18:30, 19:30, 20:30, 21:30.
- **Weekend departures:** 06:00, 07:30, 09:30, 10:00, 11:00, 12:30, 13:00, 13:30, 15:00, 15:45, 17:15, 18:00, 18:30, 19:30, 20:30, 21:30.
- **Key stops:** Larnaca Port, Jumbo (Ammochostou), New Famagusta station (Super Action), **Ayia Napa Monastery** (closest to apartment, ~10 min walk), Nissi Beach, Makronissos, Water World Waterpark.
- Return from Paralimni/Ayia Napa: similar frequency 05:55-21:15.

### Nicosia -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni
- **Price:** EUR 6 one-way, EUR 11 return, EUR 55 weekly.
- **Duration:** ~2 hrs.
- **Nicosia departure:** Solomos Square.
- Weekday departures: 07:15-21:15 (roughly every 1-2 hrs).
- Weekend departures: 08:30-21:30 (slightly less frequent).
- **Key stops:** Nicosia, New Famagusta station, **Ayia Napa Monastery**, Nissi Beach, Water World Waterpark.

### Paphos -- Larnaca -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni
- **Price:** EUR 10 one-way (full route), EUR 19 return.
- **Duration:** ~2 hrs from Larnaca.
- **One departure each direction per day:** Paralimni 07:30 → Paphos; Paphos Karavella 16:00 → Paralimni.
- Stops same as Larnaca route on the Larnaca-Ayia Napa leg.

## Pame Bus 425 (Larnaca Airport ↔ City)

Operator: Pame / Cyprus Public Transport (Larnaca urban service).

**Airport → City (frequent):**
- Every 30 min, 06:30 to midnight (36 trips/day).
- Airport → Finikoudes ("Grigori Afxentiou Ave. - Foinikoudes 2"): ~24 min.
- Airport → Arch. Makariou stop ("Archiep. Makariou C´ Ave. - Filiou Tsigaridi 1"): ~25 min.
- Route continues to Dekeleia (through the beach hotels strip).

**City → Airport (very limited):**
- Only 9 trips from Larnaca Station: 06:05, 06:35, 07:05, 07:35, 08:35, 15:20, 15:55, 16:25, 16:50.
- Not reliable for departure day unless flying morning or mid-afternoon.

## OSEA Local/Regional Buses (osea.com.cy)

Operator: OSEA (Famagusta district). Freephone: 8000 5200.
Timetable PDFs at osea.com.cy/en/bus-routes/ (updated seasonally).

### Ayia Napa area circles (local beach hopping)
- **201 (West):** Central Bus Station -- Ayia Napa Square -- Nissi Avenue -- Nissi Beach -- Makronissos Beach -- Ayia Napa Marina -- back.
- **202 (East):** Central Bus Station -- Ayia Napa Square -- Kryou Nerou -- Ammos tou Kampouri Beach -- Love Bridge -- Thalassa Museum -- back.

### Ayia Napa to Protaras / Paralimni (coastal)
- **101:** Ayia Napa Waterpark -- Cape Greco -- Konnos -- Protaras -- Pernera -- Paralimni.
- **102:** Reverse of 101.
- **501/502:** Deryneia -- Agia Thekla Beach -- Ayia Napa Marina -- Waterpark -- Ayia Napa -- Paralimni (both directions).

### Ayia Napa to Larnaca (regional, two-way)
- **711:** Paralimni -- Kapparis -- Protaras -- Konnos -- Ayia Napa Marina -- coast -- Xylofagou -- Ormideia -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way route. Also serves Municipal Library of Larnaka stop (direction: toward Larnaca). PDF: https://osea.com.cy/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/711.pdf
- **712:** Paralimni -- inland villages -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way.
- **714:** Paralimni -- Liopetri -- Dhekelia -- Larnaca (Metropolis Mall). Two-way.

**Note:** 711/712/714 terminate at Metropolis Mall, not at Finikoudes or the airport. Use InterCity + Pame 425 for airport transfers.
