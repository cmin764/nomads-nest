Getting In🔑
Self Check-In & Check-Out
→Check-in after 3 PM  ·  Check-out before 10 AM. Flexible if agreed beforehand.
→Lockbox on the right wall. Insert code, pull trigger, retrieve your key set.
⚠Big key = apartment door  ·  Small key = terrace gate. Always take your keys — door locks automatically. €50 lost key fee.
Comfort & Convenience🛋
Water & Energy
→Hot water: rooftop solar. Drag knob left, wait one minute. If cold, switch boiler on 30 min before showering — off after.
→Turn off AC when you leave. Close windows and doors while it runs.
→Wi-Fi password on the rounded table in the living area.
